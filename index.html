Tetrisnake 3D

The snake drops into a 3D well from the top. Steer it in 3D to eat food and to land where you want.

Crashing into a wall, a block, or yourself locks the snake and drops a new one — fill a full line to clear it. It's only game over when there's no room left up top.

Turn with Arrow keys or WASD. Drag / two-finger drag to rotate the view.
